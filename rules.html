Rules of CardBot Battle


CardBot battle is a turn-based card game, where to opponents with 1 card each take turns to build attack and defense, and destroy the opponent


First, two players start a battle with c.battle and c.accept (CHALLENGER USERNAME)

Second, one player is randomely chosen to go first. It does not matter who goes first, because both players go before the attack phase begins

The player who goes gets a certain amount of turns, depending on the card. If they type 1, attack will increase the the incremental amount, also designated in the Search API

Do not worry about the opponent seeing what you do, the bot deletes the message immediately

Third, the second player goes, doing the same thing

Last, the attack is subtracted from the opponents defense they had, and then dealt to them, and vice-versa.

This process repeats until 1 player's card dies

That is it!


More Info

The amount of moves a card gets depends on the rarity, unless specified otherwise

Card layout, details, or any powers are subject to change
