To view the Global Market, use the command c.global.

To find the cheapest options for a card, use c.global .

You will then be prompted with something that looks like this =

Here is what I found!

ID:__ID__ -
Seller: __USERNAME__
Price: __PRICE__

To buy, type c.buyglobal (ID)
